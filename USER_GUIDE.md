# Platform User Guide: AI Legal Assistant

Welcome to the professional-grade AI Legal Intelligence platform. Follow this guide to maximize your claim potential.

## Step 1: Claim Discovery
1.  Navigate to the **Dashboard**.
2.  Use the **Semantic Search Bar** to type keywords related to your situation (e.g., "T-Mobile breach 2024" or "Banking data leak").
3.  Review the matched cases. Each card shows an AI-generated summary and the estimated payout.

## Step 2: In-Depth Analysis
1.  Click on a **Case Card** to open the Intelligence Modal.
2.  Review the **AI Legal Reasoner** section for a simplified explanation of the legal complexities.
3.  Check the **Direct Source Link** to verify the settlement on official court websites.

## Step 3: AI Eligibility Validation
1.  Navigate to the **AI Decision Engine** tab.
2.  Input your specific details (e.g., records affected, time since breach).
3.  Click **"Predict Success"**. The platform uses a calibrated XGBoost model to provide a real-time probability of claim success.

## Step 4: Conversational Guidance
1.  Use the **AI Legal Assistant** (Chatbot) for specific Q&A.
2.  Example Questions:
    *   "What type of cases are here?"
    *   "How do I file for the AT&T settlement?"
    *   "Can you provide the URL for the Samsung data breach case?"

## Step 5: Action Plan
- Follow the **Strategic Suggestions** provided by the AI.
- Note: Your interactions are securely logged in our local database for audit purposes to ensure accuracy.
